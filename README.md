# Sales-Analysis

📊 Sales Dashboard | Microsoft Excel

An interactive Microsoft Excel Sales Dashboard designed to transform raw sales data into meaningful business insights. This project leverages Pivot Tables, Pivot Charts, Slicers, and Excel's visualization capabilities to monitor key sales metrics, customer performance, and regional trends through a dynamic and user-friendly dashboard.

🚀 Project Overview

This project analyzes sales data to provide a comprehensive overview of business performance. The dashboard enables users to explore sales by category, monitor profit trends, evaluate state-wise sales distribution, track monthly performance, and identify top customers using interactive filters.

Built entirely in Microsoft Excel, this dashboard demonstrates how Excel can be used as a powerful Business Intelligence tool for data analysis and reporting.

🎯 Business Objectives
Monitor overall sales performance across different product categories.
Analyze profit trends over multiple years.
Compare sales distribution across states.
Identify high-value customers and purchasing patterns.
Track monthly sales performance.
Enable dynamic filtering for interactive business analysis.

KPI Reporting

💡 Key Insights
Storage and Binders generated the highest sales among all product categories.
Profit showed a consistent upward trend from 2014 to 2017.
Sales performance varied significantly across different states, highlighting regional opportunities.
Monthly sales peaked during the final quarter, indicating strong seasonal demand.
A small group of customers contributed a significant share of overall sales, emphasizing the importance of customer retention.
